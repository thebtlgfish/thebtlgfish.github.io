DISCLAIMER




I think i might have to clarify about the analytics feature, more specifically how it works and what it is used for. How it works is quite simple, every time a user sends a bot command in chat, it saves that command in a txt file on my system (where the bot is running). this way i can tell what commands are being used most often and if people are using commands that do not exist frequently, i know i should add them. What i am trying to tell you is to NEVER SHARE SENSITIVE INFORMATION TO THE BOT EVER as it is archived.
